How CoolSculpting Works

CoolSculpting uses advanced cryolipolysis technology to freeze and eliminate fat cells. Here’s how the process works:

  1. Precise Cooling Application – A special applicator is placed on the targeted area to deliver controlled cooling.
  2. Fat Cells Freeze & Break Down – The cold temperature crystallizes fat cells, causing them to break down naturally.
  3. Body’s Natural Elimination Process – Over the next few weeks, the body flushes out the destroyed fat cells.
  4. Visible Results in a Few Months – As the body processes the fat, you’ll notice a slimmer, more sculpted figure.

This effective CoolSculpting Lubbock treatment is designed to contour your body in a way that looks natural and complements your overall shape.
